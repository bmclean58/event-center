Welcome to the Andover Community Center!

About Us

What began as a YMCA in 1972 has blossomed into a staple in the Andover community with the addition of its own library! We now boast two ice rinks, hosting most of the community's youth hockey events. We are so excited to announce the opening of our outdoor amphitheatre in the Spring of 2025! We look forward to hosting many live music and other events!
